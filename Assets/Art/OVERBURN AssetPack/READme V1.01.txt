---- [ASSET PACK] OVERBURN ----------------

Note : Each of the 8 Rotating Orbs comes with 3 spritesheets : 
1 full spritesheet and 2 additional spritesheets that allows you to get the orb to rotate around a character for example 
(1 sheet is in front of the character, 1 sheet is behind the character). 
Of course both of them need to be played at the same speed and you need to keep empty rects when slicing the sheets in your editor :)
